Strategy

Cross-sectional reversal on a 10-asset crypto universe.

Each 24h the runner ranks all assets by sign · log(C[t] / C[t-72h]). With sign = -1 (REV), the biggest gainers over the past 72h get the most negative score (shorted), and the biggest laggers get the most positive score (longed).

Rank hysteresis: a position is kept while its signal stays inside the top/bottom k_exit band; new legs fill gaps from the top 3.
